Unity DLSS!

We are pleased to announce the launch of Upscaler, a plugin
coming to the Unity Asset Store on October 20th, 2023! Upscaler
provides NVIDIA DLSS support to Unity Engine's Universal Render
Pipeline and Built-in Render Pipeline. Updates in the coming months
will provide additional features, so stay tuned!

Future Products

Conifer will release four plugins in the coming year, all introducing
new features to both the Unreal and Unity game engines.

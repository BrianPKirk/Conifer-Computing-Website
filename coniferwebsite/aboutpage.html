Conifer
Computing Company

Conifer Computing Company, or Conifer, was founded in the early summer of 2022 by highschool friends
Thadd Schelp, Michael Steuber, and Brian Kirk. Work proceeded on Conifer's first project, a game engine
which had been in development for two years prior. Eventually, it was deemed too big a task for Conifer's small team
and the company restructured to begin development of a new line of products - middleware - specifically plugin software
for the Unity and Unreal engines. With our new software release imminent, we are looking for programmers to join our team.

What's to come?

start and finish in the next twelve months (how exciting). All of the aforementioned projects may or may not involve the creation of cross-platform Unreal Engine and Unity Engine plugins.

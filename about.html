Conifer Computing Company

Conifer Computing Company, or Conifer, was founded in the early summer of 2022 by high school friends Thadd Schelp, Michael Steuber, and Brian Kirk. Work proceeded on Conifer's first project, but eventually, it was deemed too big a task for Conifer's small team and the company restructured to begin development on a new line of products: middleware. Specifically, plugin software for the Unity and Unreal game engines. With our new software release imminent, we are looking for programmers to join our team.

What's to come?

In the coming months, we at Conifer are set to release our first product! After that, we’ve got a roadmap of planned projects which we will start and finish in the next twelve months (how exciting). All the aforementioned projects may or may not involve the creation of cross-platform Unreal Engine and Unity Engine plugins. Eventually, we will resume work on our proprietary game engine, once we’ve accumulated enough resources to do so (finally making Thadd happy again). As stated in the first paragraph, we are currently looking to expand our team of developers in order to create our empire, so for anyone looking to join up, go to the contact page.
